'''Emblems''' are hero enhancements that grant additional buffs or effects as well as bonus attributes. In the preparation tab or during the hero pick phase, players can choose their preferred emblem sets. Currently, there are '''7''' types of emblems, each with its own set of bonuses.

'''Standard talents''' and '''core talents''' are the two of talents. Players can choose additional effects for their chosen hero via talents, providing them with an advantage with the suitable configurations; however, they can only choose two standard talents and one core talent. Currently, there are '''16''' types of standard talents and '''8''' types of core talents, each with its own set of bonuses.
